What to do

Here are some of our favorite activities in the area

  • Float the Truckee River! Truckee River Raft will rent you a raft and transport you back to your car when you reach the end.
  • Bike along the Squaw Valley bike path. You can take this all the way from Olympic Valley into Tahoe City and around the lake. Truckee River Bikes is right at the end of Olympic Valley and has rentals
  • Ride the tram to the top of Palisades mountain to get great views of Lake Tahoe

Where to Eat

  • West Shore Market is our go to sandwich spot!
  • The Rocker is right in Olympic Village is good for dinner. We love their nachos :)
  • Jake's On The Lake is a great lunch option on Lake Tahoe
